About Darkest Guides

This website is a passion project that aims to bring more people into the world of roguelikes and roguelites through games. such as Darkest Dungeon and its sequel. Darkest Dungeon 2.

The contents of this website give in-depth guides for both beginner and intermediate players, while also giving cheat sheets. and character tier lists to help new players tread the darkest dungeon.

About the designer

I am an avid fan of the roguelike genre, from Hades, Slay the Spire, Darkest Dungeons, and the like. I enjoy the difficulty and tactile design of this genre, the permanent death aspect allows the player to keep redefining new ways to combat past deaths and overcome it with a feeling of accomplishments, that feeling alone is what pulled me into the rogue like genre.

I created this website as both a university and a passion project. I have only been learning HTML through what I have been taught in lectures, and I am not a great designer. I am more interested in the coding fundamentals and technical aspects of computing, but I can still enjoy the particulars of design.
